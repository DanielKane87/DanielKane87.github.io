MY EXPERIENCE WITH AUTISM

Most people wouldn't be able to tell that I'm autistic unless I outright told them.
While I do still exhibit some autistic behaviors, such as social difficulty and comfort in isolation,
my symptoms are so negligible that I can go through life without much difficulty.

When I was little, though, I wasn't able to speak at the age that most kids should be speaking.
Thankfully, I was able to attend a special school and learned how to speak,
so now I can speak just as eloquently as anyone else.

I had to memorize all of the information I could and was allowed to use some slides as guidelines.
