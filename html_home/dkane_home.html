MY HOMETOWN

Las Vegas is the place I call home sweet home (when I'm not at university).
That's right. I'm from Sin City, the land of casinos and partying.
However, unlike what popular opinion seems to hold, I don't spend every weekend at The Strip or anything of the like.
In fact, I've probably been to The Strip once in my entire life.

Below are images featuring the most important locations in my lifetime at Las Vegas (excluding my household for obvious security reasons).



Jacobson Elementary School. I attended school here from kindergarten to 3rd grade.
After 3rd grade I was pulled out of the public school system and homeschooled until my sophomore year of high school.

Founders Classical Academy. I attended school here from sophomore year to senior year of high school.

St. Bridget's Catholic Church. I attended mass here for 19 years before the Latin Mass was switched over to the Shrine of the Holy Redeemer, featured next.

The Shrine of the Holy Redeemer. This is where my family and I currently attend Mass, and where I also sing as part of the church choir.
